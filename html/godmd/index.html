Info & Credits

This configuration UI is based on the excellent work of jeelabs esp-link (see below). Many thanks to the great team around Thorsten von Eicken.

goDMD is inspired from run-dmd and the work of Torbjörn. I also got lots of support from the german flippermarkt forum, especially Rappelbox and Gbausm, and of course to Lucky1.

There is also many content contribution from the virtual pinball community namely Toxi did a lot of work with vpinmame.

The JeeLabs esp-link firmware bridges the ESP8266 serial port to WiFi and can program microcontrollers over the serial port, in particular Arduinos, AVRs, and NXP's LPC800 and other ARM processors. Please refer to the online README for up-to-date help.
